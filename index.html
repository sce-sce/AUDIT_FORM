Part 1 of 7
Where You Are
These reveal where energy is currently stuck.
Q1
In which area of your life do you feel the most stagnation, heaviness, or repetition right now?
Health / money / relationships / purpose / work / self-trust — be specific
Q2
What problem keeps reappearing no matter how much work you do on yourself?
The one that keeps coming back. The pattern you're tired of.
Q3
If your life stayed exactly the same for the next 5 years, what scares you most?
Answer from the gut, not the mind.
Part 2 of 7
How You Stop Yourself
These reveal your execution failure pattern.
Q4
When growth or change is required, how do you tend to avoid it?
Procrastination / distraction / over-planning / staying busy / numbing / controlling
Q5
What do you start with enthusiasm and then abandon?
Be specific. Name something real.
Q6
What commitment to yourself do you keep breaking, over and over?
Not to others — to yourself.
Part 3 of 7
The Identity Underneath
These reveal the root — the self-concept driving the behavior.
Q7
What part of your current identity would have to change — or disappear — for you to reach the next level?
What version of you would need to die?
Q8
What fear feels "irrational" to you, yet still controls important decisions?
The one you know doesn't make sense but acts anyway.
Q9
What role do you tend to repeat in life — in work, relationships, and challenges?
Victim / rescuer / over-achiever / people-pleaser / avoider / rebel / the strong one
Part 4 of 7
Your Body's Signal
These ground the pattern in physical reality.
Q10
How does your body respond when you're about to grow, be seen, or take a significant step?
Tension / shutdown / hyperactivity / numbness / anxiety / avoidance — describe the physical sensation
Q11
What sensation or state do you habitually override to keep functioning?
Fatigue / emotions / needs / discomfort — what do you push past to "keep going"?
Part 5 of 7
What You Already Know
These cut to the truth the person is circling.
Q12
What truth about yourself are you tired of knowing but not acting on?
The one you've heard before. The one that's always there.
Q13
Which part of this assessment did you already want to skip or rush through?
Name it honestly.
Q14
What would change in your daily life if you stopped blaming circumstances, timing, or other people?
One specific thing.
Part 6 of 7
Your Readiness
These qualify commitment. High-intent clients answer directly. Low-intent will hedge.
Q15
Right now, are you more attached to comfort or to coherence?
Be honest. There is no wrong answer — only an accurate one.
Q16
What habits or patterns are keeping your energy low, scattered, or inconsistent?
Name at least 2 specific ones.
Q17
Why must this pattern end now — not in 6 months, not "eventually"?
What is it actually costing you? Be specific.
